The Experience

Dive into the vibrant heart of music at the Vancouver Island MusicFest brought to you by the Comox Valley Folk Society and a collective of 1,300 amazing volunteers. This unforgettable weekend of camraderie, festival magic, food and drink transforms the Courtenay Exhibition Grounds into a musical wonderland, boasting 5 to 6 dynamic stages.

Immerse yourself in an eclectic mix of Roots, Folk, and Global music, featuring a stellar lineup of artists from local gems to global icons.

This festival isn't just about music. It's a cultural odyssey with an array of workshops, jam-packed jam sessions, and soul-stirring concerts.

Our campgrounds lie along the serene Tsolum River, surrounded by lush forests. Our rustic camping experience is designed for those who share our love of nature, music and community spirit.

Comox Valley Folk Society

At our core, we celebrate diversity in our volunteers and performers, and are dedicated to an inclusive, uplifting and eco-friendly vibe. Join the movement where positivity, music, culture, and environmental consciousness converge!

Indigenous Territorial Acknowledgement

The Vancouver Island Music Festival proudly honors the vibrant heritage of the Indigenous peoples whose ancestral lands serve as the backdrop for our celebration of music, friendship, and community. We pay homage to the Puntledge, E’iksan, and K’omoks peoples, whose enduring connection to this land resonates in our festival's spirit today. Join us in a heartfelt tribute to the rich history and ongoing legacy that shapes our gathering.
